~Crystal Collector~

Instructions

You will be given a random number once the game starts. There are 4 crystals shown below. Each crystal has a hidden value. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed on the screen. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number displayed. Each time a new game starts, the value of each crystal will randomly change.
